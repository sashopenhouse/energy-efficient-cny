The Problem

CNY Winters Are Brutal.
Old Windows Make Them Expensive.

01
Drafts & Cold Spots

That chill near your windows in January isn't just discomfort — it's your furnace running nonstop every night trying to compensate for heat pouring out through the glass.

02
Heating Bills That Won't Quit

Single-pane and aging double-pane windows lose heat aggressively at -10°F windchills. The exact conditions CNY delivers every January and February.

03
Condensation & Ice on Glass

Frost forming inside your windows is a red flag — it means thermal transfer is so severe your glass is reaching dew point, which leads to mold, rot, and frame damage.

04
Incentives You're Missing

High-efficiency window upgrades may qualify for federal tax credits and NY state rebate programs, depending on product specs and program rules. Every month with old windows is money you're leaving on the table.
